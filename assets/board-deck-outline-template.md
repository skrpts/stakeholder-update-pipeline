## Board Deck Outline Template

### Deck Metadata

- **Product/Programme:** {{product_name}}
- **Reporting period:** {{reporting_period}}
- **Presenter:** {{presenter_name}}, {{presenter_role}}
- **Board meeting date:** {{meeting_date}}
- **Estimated presentation time:** 20-30 minutes + Q&A

---

### Slide 1: Title

- **Title:** {{product_name}} — Board Update, {{reporting_period}}
- **Subtitle:** {{one_line_strategic_positioning}}
- **Presenter:** {{presenter_name}}, {{presenter_role}}
- **Visual:** Clean layout with company branding

---

### Slide 2: Strategic Context

- **Key message:** {{strategic_context_headline}}
- **Content:**
  - Company vision alignment: {{vision_alignment}}
  - Active strategic pillars: {{strategic_pillars}}
  - Changes since last board meeting: {{strategic_changes}}
- **Speaker notes:** Remind the board of the strategic direction set at the previous meeting and highlight any evolution.
- **Visual:** Strategic pillar diagram or OKR alignment chart

---

### Slide 3: Headline Metrics

- **Key message:** {{metrics_headline}}
- **Content:**
  - {{kpi_1_name}}: {{kpi_1_value}} ({{kpi_1_trend}}, target: {{kpi_1_target}})
  - {{kpi_2_name}}: {{kpi_2_value}} ({{kpi_2_trend}}, target: {{kpi_2_target}})
  - {{kpi_3_name}}: {{kpi_3_value}} ({{kpi_3_trend}}, target: {{kpi_3_target}})
  - {{kpi_4_name}}: {{kpi_4_value}} ({{kpi_4_trend}}, target: {{kpi_4_target}})
- **Speaker notes:** Focus on the business significance of each metric, not the number itself. What does this mean for growth, retention, or market position?
- **Visual:** KPI dashboard with sparkline trends

---

### Slide 4: Progress Against Plan

- **Key message:** {{progress_headline}}
- **Content:**
  - Milestones completed: {{completed_milestones}}
  - Milestones on track: {{on_track_milestones}}
  - Milestones at risk: {{at_risk_milestones}}
  - Overall programme status: On Track / At Risk / Off Track
- **Speaker notes:** Connect milestone progress to the commitments made at the previous board meeting.
- **Visual:** Timeline or Gantt-style progress visualisation

---

### Slide 5: Market and Competitive Position

- **Key message:** {{market_headline}}
- **Content:**
  - Competitive moves observed: {{competitive_moves}}
  - Market trends affecting strategy: {{market_trends}}
  - Customer sentiment themes: {{customer_themes}}
- **Speaker notes:** Position the product team's work within the broader market context. Boards care about competitive moats and market timing.
- **Visual:** Competitive positioning matrix or market landscape

---

### Slide 6: Deep Dive — {{deep_dive_topic}}

- **Key message:** {{deep_dive_headline}}
- **Content:**
  - Problem/opportunity: {{deep_dive_problem}}
  - Approach taken: {{deep_dive_approach}}
  - Results achieved: {{deep_dive_results}}
  - Learnings and implications: {{deep_dive_learnings}}
- **Speaker notes:** This is the most detailed slide — use it to demonstrate strategic thinking and execution quality.
- **Visual:** Before/after comparison, funnel analysis, or impact chart

---

### Slide 7: Risks and Mitigations

- **Key message:** {{risk_headline}}
- **Content:**
  - {{risk_1}}: Severity {{risk_1_severity}}, Mitigation: {{risk_1_mitigation}}
  - {{risk_2}}: Severity {{risk_2_severity}}, Mitigation: {{risk_2_mitigation}}
  - {{risk_3}}: Severity {{risk_3_severity}}, Mitigation: {{risk_3_mitigation}}
  - Risks resolved since last meeting: {{resolved_risks}}
- **Speaker notes:** Present risks matter-of-factly. The board respects candour and is more concerned by unreported risks than by reported ones.
- **Visual:** Risk matrix (likelihood vs. impact)

---

### Slide 8: Resource and Financial Summary

- **Key message:** {{resource_headline}}
- **Content:**
  - Budget utilised: {{budget_used}} of {{budget_total}} ({{budget_percentage}}%)
  - Headcount: {{current_headcount}} / {{planned_headcount}}
  - Key investments this period: {{key_investments}}
  - Financial outlook: {{financial_outlook}}
- **Speaker notes:** Boards want to know money is being spent wisely. Connect investments to expected returns.
- **Visual:** Budget waterfall or resource allocation stacked bar

---

### Slide 9: Looking Ahead

- **Key message:** {{outlook_headline}}
- **Content:**
  - Top priorities for next quarter: {{next_quarter_priorities}}
  - Expected milestones: {{next_milestones}}
  - Strategic decisions upcoming: {{upcoming_decisions}}
- **Speaker notes:** This slide sets the agenda for the next board meeting. Make commitments you intend to report against.
- **Visual:** Quarterly roadmap or priority stack

---

### Slide 10: Asks of the Board

- **Key message:** "We need the following from the board"
- **Content:**
  - {{ask_1}}: {{ask_1_context}} — Recommended action: {{ask_1_recommendation}}
  - {{ask_2}}: {{ask_2_context}} — Recommended action: {{ask_2_recommendation}}
- **Speaker notes:** Be specific about what you need. "Support" is not an ask. "Approve a $200K increase to the Q3 marketing budget to fund the enterprise launch campaign" is an ask.
- **Visual:** Clean text layout with clear action items

---

### Appendix Slides (as needed)

- Detailed metric tables
- Customer research summaries
- Technical architecture diagrams
- Financial projections with assumptions
